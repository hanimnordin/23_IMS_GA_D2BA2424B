History

Froogurtz was founded in early November 2020, and the company's initial location, which is now located in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, is the first froogurtz in Austhetic. Froogurtz is a Malaysian company that makes the first yoghurt ice cream. Mohammad Hafizuddin Che Yusoff, a Kelantan local who has been interested in ice cream yoghurt since he studied at UiTM Shah Alam, is the creator of Froogurtz.
